Product Design: Ad Creative Management Interface
The Rubicon Project

To accommodate the evolving ecosystem of online advertising, more fluid assets were introduced into the ad format line up. This led our team to create interfaces that our Account Managers could use to produce customizable dynamic and native ads for clients. Both interfaces launched in 2015 on the Rubicon Dashboard.
Native Creative
This interface was designed to give the user the ability to create native ads. This type of ad arranges multiple text and image assets into a composition that resembles the natural flow of the content in which the ad is placed.
Dynamic Creative
Dynamic ads present a moving carousel or grid of products to the consumer based on items viewed in the past. The template shown here allows clients to create a custom dynamic ad without having to wait on a creative team to execute it. Clients can upload their logo of choice, select the number of products to show, choose a font, and much more.
