Rubicon Dashboard: Creatives
Over the past few years, online advertising formats have changed drastically to reflect and compete with the constant flow of information that customers consume every day. Native and dynamic ads were introduced into our dashboard in 2015.
1. Native Creative
This interface was designed to give the user the ability to create native ads. This type of ad arranges multiple text and image assets into a composition that resembles the natural flow of the context it is placed in.
2. Dynamic Creative
Dynamic ads present a moving carousel or grid of products to the consumer based on items viewed in the past. The template shown here allows clients to create a custom dynamic ad without having to wait on a creative team to execute it. Clients can upload their logo of choice, select the amount of products to show, choose a font, and much more.
