Rubicon Dashboard: Cross Device Reporting
The cross device report provides the client with insight on the spending behaviours of their customers in regard to their products. The client can see how many times a user viewed a product before they bought it, on which device each impression occurred, and the conversion device itself.
Cross Device Reporting
The graph visualizes the amount of impressions before converting and the chart beside it captures the most popular paths to conversion. Each device is also broken down by performance in a detailed report.
