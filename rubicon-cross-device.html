Product Design: Cross-Device Reporting
The Rubicon Project

The intention of creating the cross-device report was to provide clients with visually engaging insights into the browsing and spending behaviours of their customers across all device types. The client can see how many times a user viewed a product before they bought it, on which device each impression occurred, and the conversion device itself.
Path & Performance Breakdown
The graph visualizes the number of impressions before converting and the chart beside it captures the most popular paths to conversion. Each device is also broken down by performance in a detailed report.
