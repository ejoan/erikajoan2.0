Product Design: Audience Forecasting
The Rubicon Project

This new feature in the Rubicon Dashboard was implemented to allow users to determine the potential reach of future advertising campaigns. The audience forecast is established based on a set of parameters users are asked to specify, such as audience segment, inventory type, and region.
Forecasting Interface
Once the user has specified all of the desired parameters, they can press the calculate button to see the estimated reach.
