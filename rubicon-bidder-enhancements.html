Product Design: Enterprise Bidder Enhancements
The Rubicon Project

When the Rubicon Project Enterprise Bidder (RPEB) first came to be, it was a project spearheaded by a team of engineers. While they built a powerful product, there were some crucial user experience problems, a lack of branding, and no design consistency. This project shows steps we took to begin introducing more user-friendly elements into the platform. Although it hasn't gone through a complete transition, some small improvements have created a much more enjoyable experience.
Navigation
Adding brand colours and a context appropriate logo was the first of many changes to create a big visual impact on the Enterprise Bidder.

Buttons
The button updates included creating copy consistency, removal of icons, and a style change to bring them up to date.
  • Outcome

Reports
Similar to the older buttons, the report titles included icons that were not necessary. Hamburger icons used to sit to the left of the report title for no apparent reason. The report title now has breathing room, statuses are colour-coded for easy reading, column headers are title case, and the highlighted row colour is easier on the eyes.
